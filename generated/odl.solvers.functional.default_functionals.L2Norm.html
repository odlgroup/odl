L2Norm¶

class odl.solvers.functional.default_functionals.L2Norm(*args, **kwargs)[source]¶

Bases: LpNorm

The functional corresponding to the L2-norm.

The L2-norm, ||x||_2, is defined as the square-root out of the integral/sum of x^2.
