RectPartition.is_uniform_byaxis¶

property RectPartition.is_uniform_byaxis¶

Boolean tuple showing uniformity of self.grid per axis.

Examples

>>> part = nonuniform_partition([0, 1, 3], [1, 2, 3])
>>> part.is_uniform_byaxis
(False, True)
