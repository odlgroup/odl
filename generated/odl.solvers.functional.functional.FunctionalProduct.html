Examples

Construct the functional || . ||_2^2 * 3

>>> space = odl.rn(2)
>>> func1 = odl.solvers.L2NormSquared(space)
>>> func2 = odl.solvers.ConstantFunctional(space, 3)
>>> prod = odl.solvers.FunctionalProduct(func1, func2)
>>> prod([2, 3])  # expect (2**2 + 3**2) * 3 = 39
39.0
